(Acharya Ravishenaru 1400 years ago wrote Padma purana. In that, in the 32nd parva, he describes the greatness of doing abhisheka of thirthankara pratima with milk, curd, ghee, sugarcane juice and water.)

Upadesha on Gruhasta Dharma:
3 times darshana for Nirmala Bhava.
Kamala, Ketaki, Malathi, Mogra, Juhi, Chameli, Champa, Gulabi aadi suganda hoovininda pooje maadthare - Pushpaka Vimana annu prapta madi kondu kreede madthara

Agara Chandana Doopa haktare: Suganda Sharira prapti madi kolluthare...

Deepa : Dodda ruddhi dhari devate deepada reeti sharira prapta madi kolluthare

Mangala Dravya (Chatra, Chamara, Dwaja, Darpana, ... ) Koduthare, and  Mandiravannu Shringara madthara , and Jala chandanadinda pooje madthare --- avaru devigala swami and maha nirmala shariravanna prapti madikondu apsareyara pati aguthare

Jala abhisheka maduvavaru --- Chakravarthi aguthare
Haalinda abhisheka - Ksheera sagara samana ujjwala vimanadalli parama kanti dhari deva agi utpatti aguthare, and manushya bhava prapti madi kondu parampare inda moksha hoguthare.
Mosaru --- Mosarina sama ujjwala yashavannu prapti madi kondu samsari roopi samudradinda paaru haaguthare.
Tuppa - Swargadalli maha riddhi dhari deva agi hutti parampare inda ananta virya (kevala jnaana) prapti aguthade.
Ikshu rasa - Amrutada aahara maduva indra aagi, nareshwara (chakravarthi) agi, avinashwara padavi prapta madi kolluthare.
Abhisheka prabhavadinda bhavya jivi galige devendra naagi avanaige abhisheka prapti aguthade.
