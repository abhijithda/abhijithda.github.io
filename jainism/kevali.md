# Kevalis

Reference: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KzJo0Hmfi28>

## 7 Types of kevalis

1. Thirthankar kevali
2. Samanya kevali
3. Mook kevali
4. Upasarg kevali
5. Antakrut kevali
6. Samudhghat kevali
7. Anubaddh kevali

3rd shukla dhyan - Sayog kevali.
4th shukla dhyan - Ayog kevali.
